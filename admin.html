Setup Instructions

  1. Go to Bitrix24: Open your Bitrix24 account and navigate to: Menu → Developer Resources → Other → Inbound Webhook
  2. Assign Permissions: Click the "select" button in the "Assign permissions" section
  3. Select CRM Scope: From the list, check the box next to "CRM" (this grants access to all CRM methods including leads, deals, and activities)
  4. Save: Click "Save" and then click the green "SAVE" button at the bottom
  5. Copy URL: Copy your webhook URL and paste it below
Note: By selecting the "CRM" scope, you automatically get access to all CRM operations (crm.lead.add, crm.lead.update, crm.deal.add, crm.activity.add, etc.)
